# **Wendi Kaspar (2020). Back to School?. *College & Research Libraries*, Vol. 81, No. 6. https://doi.org/10.5860/crl.81.6.894**

As I write this September editorial (a few weeks before publication), I can’t help but wonder what the world of higher education and of academic libraries will look like when the issue is released. Right now, it is the rush to the fall semester. Some institutions are holding virtual-only classes while others are planning face-to-face classes or some combination thereof. There are also institutions not opening at all and that will never open again due to the economic impacts of the pandemic.
