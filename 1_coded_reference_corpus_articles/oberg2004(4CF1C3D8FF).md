# **Dianne Oberg (2004). Editorial: The Synergy of Making Connections. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 10, No. 1. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6948**

"Making connections" was one of the themes that emerged during the doctoral defense of a student at the University of Alberta this week. It was the doctoral defense that brought Julie Tallman from the University of Georgia to Edmonton, and we are taking the opportunity of that event in our work as supervisors of graduate students to share our work as editors of School Libraries Worldwide. Most of our editorial work related to the journal is conducted over the Internet and the telephone lines. Rarely do we get the chance to work in the same office, to share conversations over meals, and to laugh and enjoy the company of old and new friends.
