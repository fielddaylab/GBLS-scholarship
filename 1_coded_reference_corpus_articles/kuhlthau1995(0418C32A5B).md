### Summary
# **Carol Collier Kuhlthau (1995). The Process of Learning from Information. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 1, No. 1. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6939**

This article presents the process of learning from information as the key concept for the library media center in the information age school. The Information Search Process Approach, based on Kuhlthau's research, is described as a model for developing information skills that are fundamental to information literacy. Some basic abilities for using information that even the youngest children can learn are recommended along with strategies for implementing this approach.
