## Abstract

In this last issue of the volume year, it is a good time to communicate some changes that we will be implementing to the journal. Some may be more welcome than others but all of them have been considered and discussed with the Editorial Board and ALA Production staff with the hope that it will provide a more streamlined process for the journal without compromising the experience of the readers or authors.
