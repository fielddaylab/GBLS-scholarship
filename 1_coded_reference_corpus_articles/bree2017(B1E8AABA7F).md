### Summary
# **Hans Bree, Gunter Schlamp (2017). A Regional German School Library Association: A Story of Success?. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 23, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6926**

This article describes the history of a regional German school library working group (LAG) from 1987 to 2017 founded by a group of teachers and school in the federal state of Hesse to advise the governemnt. It was questionable if a private association could in the long term be a viable solution for the development of an underdeveloped library system but it has been successful.
