## Abstract

Training Library Instructors, edited by Matthew Weirick Johnson, is a two-part series of booksthat present a current view of instructional teaching in librarianship. A Guide to Training GraduateStudents (volume 1) and A Guide to Training Librarians (volume 2) give insightful perspectiveson the different ways that instruction is relevant to librarianship. This set is an informativemanual for those who are looking to further their understanding of teaching instruction forearly career and emerging librarians
