# **Krista Graham, Alison Grodzinski (2001). Defining the Remote Library User: An Online Survey. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 1, No. 3. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2001.0041**

An online survey was administered to assess the needs of the Central Michigan University Library's remote population. Results indicate that most users access resources remotely; however, they also continue to use the physical library. Services can be designed to target the remote user group but traditional services should not be abandoned.
