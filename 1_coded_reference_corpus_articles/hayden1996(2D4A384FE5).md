### Summary
# **Ruth Hayden (1996). A Book in the Hand is Worth Two on a Disk. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 2, No. 1. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw7001**

This article presents the argument that the school librarians need to make critical judgements with respect to the inclusion of technology in their elementary school libraries. Two concerns in particular are addressed: how technology may change the reading culture of young children and how business technological interests should be cautiously interpreted when children's reading development is considered.
