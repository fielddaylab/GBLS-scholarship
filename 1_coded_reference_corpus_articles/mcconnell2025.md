## Abstract

Amid efforts to ban materials, defund programs, and criminalize the work of librarians, this book offers a roadmap for advocacy by strengthening the connections between libraries of all types, library stakeholders, and policymakers. Even with the ongoing escalations in the political climate and the intensification of challenges to equitable access to information following the book’s publication—exemplified by the recent dismantling of the IMLS
