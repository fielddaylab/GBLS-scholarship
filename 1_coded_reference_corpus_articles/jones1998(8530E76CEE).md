### Summary
# **Paula Jones (1998). School Libraries in the South Pacific Island Nations: An Update Report. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 4, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw7057**

The Pacific Island countries face many similar problems in the provision of adequate and appropriate school library services. Education in these countries is still developing, and school libraries are not viewed as integral components of learning. Funding is insufficient, if at all, and there is it general lack of support library services. This article updates Joe Hallein's 1991 report on Cook Islands, Fiji, Samoa, Solomon Islands, Tonga, and Vanuatu. Niue Is included for the first time.
