# **Bryn Geffert (2021). In Pursuit of My Book. *College & Research Libraries*, Vol. 82, No. 6. https://doi.org/10.5860/crl.82.6.786**

I want a copy of my book. I once had print copies. My publisher kindly sent me 10 gratis. I gave most to friends and family. None of those recipients—but for my loyal father and earnest brother-in-law—read them. Yet demand exists in more remote and specialized realms. I recall the destitute graduate student in Siberia who, several years ago, wrote me asking for an electronic copy of my previous book. Having none, I shelled out $60 to Amazon for a print copy and then shipped that copy at my expense across the Urals.
