### Summary
# **Kolawole Aramide, Sunday Ladipo (2016). Training for School Librarianship Development in Nigeria: How Have Nigerian School Library Association Conference Programmes Fared?. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 22, No. 1. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6903**

This study reported the results of investigation into the relevance and importance of school library conferences to school librarians. Findings from the study revealed that school librarians do not attend school library conference regularly. Institutional invitation by posting was found to be major and most effective source of disseminating school library conference information. The study further revealed that school librarians affirmed paper presentation and interactive session as the most important aspects of school library conference while hands on/practical, organisation, time management and venue/location arrangement were considered as aspects that needed to be improved upon.
