## Abstract

A synthesis of the second progress report submitted by the Library of Congress to the Council on Library Resources under a grant for the RECON Pilot Project. An overview of the progress made from November 1969 to April 1970 in the following areas: production, Official Catalog comparison, format recognition, research titles, microfilming, investigation of inptut devices. In addition, the status of the tasks assigned to the RECON Working Task Force are briefly described.
