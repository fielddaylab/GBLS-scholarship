# **Ronald Rousseau (2016). Citation data as a proxy for quality or scientific influence are at best PAC (probably approximately correct). *Journal of the Association for Information Science and Technology (JASIST)*, Vol. 67, No. 12. https://doi.org/10.1002/asi.23525**

In this communication I give a brief introduction to V aliant's probably approximately correct ( PAC) theory, provide an extension that goes beyond V aliant's ideas (and beyond the domain for which this theory was meant), and come to an interpretation in terms of research evaluation. As such, PAC provides a framework for a theory of research evaluation.
