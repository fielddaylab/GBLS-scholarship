# **Nery Alcivar-Estrella (2025). Creating an Inclusive Library: Approaches for Increasing Engagement and Use with Students of Color. *College & Research Libraries*, Vol. 86, No. 4. https://doi.org/10.5860/crl.86.4.682**

Edited by Ngoc-Yen Tran, Michael J. Aguilar II, and Adriana Poo, Creating an Inclusive Library: Approaches for Increasing Engagement and Use with Students of Color showcases how librarians and library workers from various colleges and universities prioritize the information needs of students of color. The book addresses how to manage concerns that impact students of color through library instruction, collection development, and community building.
