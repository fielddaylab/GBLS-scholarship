# **Donna Packer (2001). Acquisitions Allocations: Fairness, Equity and Bundled Pricing. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 1, No. 3. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2001.0048**

This paper examines the effect of an interdisciplinary, web-based, citation database with full text on a library's acquisitions allocation plan. List price of full-text journals in the electronic database is used to calculate increases in acquisitions funding. The increases show unplanned, unexpected results.
