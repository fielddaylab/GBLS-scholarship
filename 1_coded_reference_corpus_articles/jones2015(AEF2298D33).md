# **Jami Jones, Barbara Flohr, Mimi Martin (2015). Theories Holding Promise for Supporting the Constructivist Behaviors of Inquiry. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 21, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6898**

The problem under investigation is how to nurture the exploratory and constructive behaviors of inquiry. To this end, the research, concepts, and theories of Sugata Mitra, Jacob Getzels, and Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi hold promise for developing opportunities for these pursuits. Additionally, research about the negative effects of reward informs school librarians who practice this creativity killer.
