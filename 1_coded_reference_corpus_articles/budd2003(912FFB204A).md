### Summary
# **John Budd, Corrie Christensen (2003). Social Sciences Literature and Electronic Information. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 3, No. 4. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2003.0077**

Most colleges and universities have expanded, and are expanding, access to electronic information resources. A question remains: to what extent are researchers making use of these resources as part of their inquiry and publishing activities? The present study investigates citation practices by authors of journal articles and includes a study of the potential incorporation of full-text and full-image resources into published work in eight social science disciplines. It also includes a brief survey of a sample of authors of the examined journal articles.
