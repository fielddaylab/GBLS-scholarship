# **Susan Clephane (2014). New Zealand school librarians: Technology leaders?. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 20, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6876**

This research examines what role and views New Zealand school librarians currently have within their schools as technology leaders. The results indicate there is a distinct hierarchy that affected the way librarians conducted their jobs and the place they see they hold within the school as a technology leaders. The implication for schools and their lib rarians may affect how librarians will fill or fail to fill possible roles for school libraries in the future.
