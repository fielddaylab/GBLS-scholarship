# **Allison Hosier (2024). The Role of Research in Creative Writing: A Follow-up Study. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 24, No. 4. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2024.a938743**

Abstract: A previous study of creative writers' self-reports revealed important differences between research as it is carried out in the context of creative writing and the more scholarly types of research academic librarians often focus on. However, that study left many questions open. For this follow-up study, the author interviewed published creative writers about the role of research in their creative process. This study offers new insight into creative writers' attitudes toward the use of creative license. Though the sample was relatively small, the findings support the results of the original research.
