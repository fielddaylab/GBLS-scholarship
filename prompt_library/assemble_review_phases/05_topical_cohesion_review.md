# Bounded Topical-Cohesion Review

Run in a fresh context. Read the shared contract and `run_status.md`.

Read only:

- `stage_4_terminology_introduction_draft.md`;
- `structure_manifest.md`;
- compact evidence limitations and outliers from section ledgers.

Do not read the complete coded corpus.

Review one H1 block at a time while keeping the complete manuscript available
for transitions. For each block:

1. identify its collective message;
2. flag author-led openings and one-source-per-sentence sequences;
3. flag broad claims supported by one source;
4. group compatible evidence around topic claims;
5. preserve real disagreements, methods, contexts, null findings, and
   necessary attribution;
6. remove article-level detail that does not change interpretation.

Do not manufacture synthesis by attaching unrelated citations to a broad
claim. If the available prose lacks enough evidence for a needed synthesis,
record the gap for phase 6 rather than inventing it.

Save `stage_5_topical_cohesion_draft.md`. In
`phase_ledgers/phase_05.md`, record one concise entry per H1 block with the
collective message, changes, retained author-led exceptions, and phase-6
evidence needs. Verify headings, citations, word count, and checksum. Advance
`run_status.md` to phase 6 and stop.
