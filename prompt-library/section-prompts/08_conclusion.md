## Required Structure

# Conclusion

## Target

Approximately 500 words.

## Section Task

Answer what this literature collectively teaches about Games-Based Library Services. Restate the service-ecology contribution without repeating the introduction. Synthesize the strongest convergent findings, the most important tensions and outliers, the conditions for success, and the limits of current evidence.

Write directly to the implications for librarians, directors, educators, partners, and governing bodies. Emphasize purpose before format, multidimensional access, facilitation, patron leadership, institutionalized expertise, sustainable infrastructure, and outcome-aligned assessment.

End with disciplined ambition: games merit serious cultural and professional attention, libraries are unusually positioned to provide public-interest infrastructure around them, and the next stage requires stronger comparative and community evidence rather than uncritical advocacy.
