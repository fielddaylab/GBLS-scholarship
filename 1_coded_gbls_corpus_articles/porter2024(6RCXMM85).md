These resources are presented as tools to help librarians navigate the complexities of securing funding for technology and programming initiatives.

The policy includes directives for moderators and encourages private communication for sensitive issues, reflecting professional standards for online community moderation.

The document concludes with contact information and links to further resources, including the GameRT blog, Facebook pages, and public resource repositories.
