### Summary
# **Margaret Mackey, Ingrid Johnston (1996). The Book Resisters: Ways of Approaching Reluctant Teenage Readers. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 2, No. 1. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6999**

Large numbers of teenagers choose not to read for many reasons. To foster reading as a creative pastime, librarians and teachers need to ask some pertinent questions. What is it that such reluctant readers need to know about reading? What do their librarians and teachers need to know? What can schools do to encourage reading? What kinds of books may be best suited to attracting those who normally do not read for pleasure? In this article, we explosure such questions and suggest some answers.
