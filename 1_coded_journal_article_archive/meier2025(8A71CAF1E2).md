# **John J. Meier (2025). Inclusive Leadership: Perspectives From Academic Library Leaders. *College & Research Libraries*, Vol. 86, No. 3. https://doi.org/10.5860/crl.86.3.506**

This paper presents the results of thirty-seven interviews, conducted with deans and university librarians at large, research institutions in the US, regarding how they practice inclusive leadership and develop future leaders from diverse backgrounds. A demographic survey shows this cohort has become increasingly representative of the profession in gender and racial identities.
