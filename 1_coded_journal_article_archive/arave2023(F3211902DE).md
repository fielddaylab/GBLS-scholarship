# **G. Arave (2023). Teaching Business Information Literacy . Genifer Snipes, Marlinda Karo, Ash E. Faulkner, and Lauren Reiter, eds. Chicago, IL: ACRL Press, 2022. 412 p. Paper, $98 (ISBN: 978-0-8389-3909-3).. *College & Research Libraries*, Vol. 84, No. 1. https://doi.org/10.5860/crl.84.1.156**

A collection of forty-one contributed chapters organized into nine topical sections, Teaching Business Information Literacy offers something for everyone engaged in library instruction in the contexts of business and entrepreneurship programs. From basic business research to more niche subjects, the chapters detail instructional approaches for a wide variety of topics and situations, with plenty of suggested modifications to further tailor each instructional session to suit the reader’s circumstances.
