### Summary
# **Lance Lugar, Kate Thomes (2002). Access to Scholarly Communication Information on ARL Member Library Websites. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 2, No. 1. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2002.0018**

This study examines ARL member library websites to assess the prominence and extent of scholarly communication information available. Data show that for nearly half of the libraries no results were retrieved using a site search function. Only 4 percent of the libraries raise these issues prominently on their websites' homepages.
