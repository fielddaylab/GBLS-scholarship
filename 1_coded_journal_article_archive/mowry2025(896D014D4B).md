Toxic Dynamics: Disrupting, Dismantling, and Transforming Academic Library Culture , edited by Russell Michalak, Trevor A. Dawes, and Jon E. Cawthorne, is a comprehensive examination of the pervasive issue of toxic workplaces within academic libraries. Each chapter is authored by different contributors, bringing a rich diversity of perspectives and expertise to the subject. The book’s primary argument is that toxic dynamics in academic libraries are a significant barrier to effective and healthy work environments.
