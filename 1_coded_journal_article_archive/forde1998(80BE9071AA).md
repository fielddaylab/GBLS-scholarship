# **Janet Lynch Forde (1998). School Libraries in St. Lucia: A State-of-the-Art Report. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 4, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw7055**

This article provides some background on factors that have affected the development of school libraries in the West Indian Island. The brief history of that development process is followed by a description of the situation as it stands today. Recent initiate tips of the local professional association to work with government officials seem to hold promise for the future.
