### Summary
# **Linda B Alexander, Robert C Smith (2001). Research Findings of a Library Skills Instruction Web Course. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 1, No. 3. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2001.0033**

This causal-comparative, quasi-experimental study asked the question: In a university library skills credit course, how does a group of traditionally-instructed students compare to a group of web-based instructed students in skills level, satisfaction, and demographics? Analyses of the data suggest that nontraditional students preferred the web format over a traditional course.
