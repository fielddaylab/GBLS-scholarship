### Summary
# **Angela K Horne, Terry L Kristensen (2004). The Development of MyContents, an Enriched Electronic Tables of Contents Service. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 4, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2004.0027**

In August 2002, Cornell University Library (CUL) launched MyContents, a campus-wide table of contents (TOC) service. This article describes how a cross-functional team of librarians and technical staff developed and implemented MyContents. It discusses the service's key features, addresses patron response, details the latest user enhancements, and conveys our vision for the future of the service.
