# **Vita Mozuraite (1999). A Golden Age and a Stone Age of School Libraries in Lithuania. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 5, No. 1. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw7064**

This article describes two eras of school library work in Lithuania -- the last two decades of Soviet times (1970-1990) and the first years of independence (1990-1997). These two periods are very different, but we can learn much from both of them.
