# **Angela Weiler (2001). Two-Year College Freshmen and the Internet: Do They Really "Know All That Stuff?". *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 1, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2001.0032**

Freshman English classes were surveyed to determine the students' computer experiences before coming to college. More than half the students surveyed had used the Internet once a week or less, and only about half had received any kind of computer training. These results are attributed to a variety of factors.
