# **Editorial Team Anonymous (1999). An Introduction to the Library Power Program. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 5, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6763**

Library Power is a school improvement initiative launched by the DeWitt Wallace-Reader's Digest Fund in 1988. Total investment in the program since its inception exceeds $45 million. Operating in approximately 700 schools and serving more than 1 million students annually, Library Power is the largest nongovernmental funding of school library services in over 30 years.
