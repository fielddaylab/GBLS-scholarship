### Summary
# **Adrian W Alexander (2001). Whither Fair Use? A Library Consortium Viewpoint. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 1, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2001.0013**

The purpose of this paper is to provide a perspective on the position of library consortia with respect to the question of fair use and the Internet. My focus will be on the policy implications for both academic libraries and the institutions of higher education that they serve. As you will see, libraries and library consortia are becoming increasingly active in this arena, as both "consumer advocates" in the marketplace and as change agents actively involved in alternative publishing initiatives that one day could change the "rules of engagement" entirely.
