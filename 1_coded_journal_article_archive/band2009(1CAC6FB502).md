# **Jonathan Band (2009). The Three P's: A Tribute to Duane Webster. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 9, No. 3. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.0.0064**

Duane Webster's leadership in the area of copyright policy demonstrates three characteristics: perseverance, pragmatism, and partnership. These characteristics are visible in the foundation and operation of the Library Copyright Alliance (LCA). LCA's successful campaign to defeat a legislative initiative in the 110th Congress to increase copyright statutory damages reflects these three characteristics.
