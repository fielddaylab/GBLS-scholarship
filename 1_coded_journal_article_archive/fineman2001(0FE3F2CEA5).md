# **Yale Fineman (2001). DW3 Classical Music Resources: Managing Mozart on the Web. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 1, No. 4. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2001.0067**

This paper discusses the genesis and development of DW3 (Duke World Wide Web) Classical Music Resources, a vertical portal that comprises the most comprehensive collection of classical music resources on the Web with links to more than 2,800 carefully selected, non-commercial pages/sites in over a dozen languages.
