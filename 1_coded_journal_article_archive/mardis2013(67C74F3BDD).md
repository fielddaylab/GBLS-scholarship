This issue of School Libraries Worldwide is based on the theme of Outliers: School Librarianship Enables Success in All Environments. Outliers are all around us-they are moguls, geniuses, pop stars, and everyday people who have made good for themselves. If an outlier is defined as a person who succeeds against the odds, how do school librarians throughout the world enable and ensure this success?
