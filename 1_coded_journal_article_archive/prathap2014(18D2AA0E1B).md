# **Gangan Prathap (2014). The zynergy-index and the formula for the h-index. *Journal of the Association for Information Science and Technology (JASIST)*, Vol. 65, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.1002/asi.23046**

The h‐index, as originally proposed ( H irsch, 2005), is a purely heuristic construction. B urrell (2013) showed that efforts to derive formulae from the mathematical framework of Lotkaian informetrics could lead to misleading results. On this note, we argue that a simple heuristic “thermodynamical” model can enable a better three‐dimensional (3 D ) evaluation of the information production process leading to what we call the zynergy‐index.
