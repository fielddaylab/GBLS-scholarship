### Summary
# **Cameron Kim Tuai (2003). Using the Concept of Teamwork to Increase Why-Based Questioning in the Academic Reference Interview. *portal: Libraries and the Academy*, Vol. 3, No. 4. https://doi.org/10.1353/pla.2003.0097**

This article proposes that within the academic reference interview why-based questions are handled inefficiently. This is a symptom of a larger issue that involves the interaction between fulfillment of the patron request and the roles assumed by patron and librarian. Actively envisioning the reference interview on a team model creates roles that are more conducive for both why-based questioning and patron satisfaction.
