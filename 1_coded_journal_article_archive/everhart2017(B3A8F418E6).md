# **Nancy Everhart (2017). Education for Teacher Librarians in Germany. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 23, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6931**

This study identifies human perceptions and concerns regarding readiness for teacher library education in Germany (where it currently does not exist) by measuring the perceptions of 183 library science educators, library school students, and librarians in Berlin, Potsdam and Leipzig.
