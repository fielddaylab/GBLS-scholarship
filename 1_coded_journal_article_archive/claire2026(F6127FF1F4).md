Training Library Instructors, edited by Matthew Weirick Johnson, is a two-part series of books that present a current view of instructional teaching in librarianship. A Guide to Training Graduate Students (volume 1) and A Guide to Training Librarians (volume 2) give insightful perspectives on the different ways that instruction is relevant to librarianship. This set is an informative manual for those who are looking to further their understanding of teaching instruction for early career and emerging librarians
