# **Heike Lammers-Harlander (2017). Embracing the Future and the Past -- A New Model for German School Libraries. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 23, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6928**

Describing the Donauwoerthian Intra-Institutional Network, a cooperative of the public library and two school libraries, this article visualizes possibilities and boundaries of creative and successful educational teamwork and presents a model for an altered school library system in Germany, closely related to international paradigms.
