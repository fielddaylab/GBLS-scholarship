# **Dianne Oberg (2006). School Libraries Worldwide Going Online!. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 12, No. 1. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw6982**

The article announces that the periodical "School Libraries Worldwide" is going online. Karen Bonnano has taken over the technical support role for the website. Anne Clyde originally designed the website for the online school libraries but unfortunately passed away on September 18, 2005. Bonnano will be helping the executives and editor of the journal to move forward on the online initiative. The journal will be published in two formats: a hard copy and an online electronic copy mounted on the website of IASL's School Libraries Online.
