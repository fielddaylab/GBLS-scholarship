### Summary
# **Leslie Farmer (2001). Teaming Parents with Technology. *School Libraries Worldwide*, Vol. 7, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.29173/slw7095**

As schools incorporate technology into the curriculum, as well as use technology for productivity and other administrative tasks, issues of parent involvement in the lives of students become even more complex and problematic. Farmer discusses the need for parents to become involved in the learning process of students, as well as ways that librarians can team parents with technology to improve student learning.
