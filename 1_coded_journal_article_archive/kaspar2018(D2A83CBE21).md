# **Wendi Arant Kaspar (2018). Authorship Norms: Credit and Roles. *College & Research Libraries*, Vol. 79, No. 2. https://doi.org/10.5860/crl.79.2.154**

As a journal editor, I regularly receive questions about authorship policies and practices, both in a general context and specific to the journal. The variety of questions and cases are mind-boggling. While there has been a lot of attention paid to the topic of authorship over the years, in both the scholarly literature and the more mainstream discourse in higher education, none of it seems to alleviate the confusion around this issue (my own included).
