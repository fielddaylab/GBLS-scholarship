## Abstract

Abstract: Though the issue of knowledge management (KM) education is not new in the KM literature, there is not much research on KM education in Russia. There is only one thorough study, published in 2018, that represented what KM education was in this country by that year. There is a lack of case studies on KM courses taught in Russian universities. This paper reviews the current situation with KM education in Russia and focuses primarily on KM as a bachelor’s and master’s course in Russian universities. It demonstrates the way KM has developed since the late 1990s when Russian research began to increase in a variety of fields. The Russian database eLIBRARY. ru and the internet were searched for KM courses and courses that included KM as a topic. This paper presents a range of KM courses that were taught from 2011 to 2022 and the fields of studies that included these courses.
