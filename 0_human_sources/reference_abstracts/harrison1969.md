## Abstract

A system to compile programs of recorded music for broadast by the University of Iowa's radio stations. The system also provides a permanent catalog of all recorded music holdings and an accurate inventory control. The program, which operates on an IBM 360/65, is available in FORTRAN IV, COBOL and PL/1, with assembly language subroutines and external maintenance programs.
