Panelists Russell Brandon (he/him) is the Data Services Librarian at the Fant Memorial Library and the Director of the Kossen Center for Teaching & Learning at the Mississippi University for Women. He teaches in the Digital Studies program and promotes data literacy on campus. Before coming to The W, Russell taught psychology at Mississippi State University and has previously worked as a middle school computer teacher. He thinks play is pretty much the best way to learn
anything. More at russellbrandon.info.

Danielle Costello (she/her) is an academic librarian at University of Georgia where she teaches library instruction and develops programming for the student body. She is also President-Elect for ALA’s Games and Gaming Roundtable where she helps to develop programming and resources to bring games and play into all libraries. Outside of work she moonlights as a cleric and occasionally as a capybara trying to maintain a healthy low stress high mayhem lifestyle.
Molly Porter (she/her) is the Adult and Teen Services Librarian at the West Feliciana Parish Library in Saint Francisville, Louisiana. She created the library’s board game collection, and she hosts board game nights and runs Dungeons and Dragons and other TTRPGs for their patrons. Molly has an MA in English literature, an MLIS, and a certification in archival studies from Louisiana State University. She serves on the Awards Committee and co-chairs the Programming Committee for the ALA Games and Gaming Round Table. In her free time, she plays as many games as her cat will allow.
Rebecca Strang (she/they) is a Children’s Services Librarian at Naperville Public Library in Illinois where she runs monthly gaming programs like Family Game Night, Video Game Club, Pokemon Club, and Adventurers’ Club (a kids RPG club). She is also the Past President of GameRT and has previously taught Game Studies at Loyola University Chicago. Rebecca believes play and storytelling are essential and strives to carve places for both in her communities. In her free time, she hangs out with her partner and their cats, plays games, sends snail mail, and drinks tea. Find out more at https://toplayishuman.com/.
